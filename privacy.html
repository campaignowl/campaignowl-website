Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

CampaignOwl is a paid advertising account management tool developed and operated in the United Kingdom. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and service.

What data we collect

When you contact us via email, we collect your email address and the content of your message. When you use the CampaignOwl service, we access your advertising account data through official APIs provided by Google Ads and Microsoft Ads.

The advertising data we access includes:

  • Campaign, ad group, and keyword performance metrics
  • Search term reports
  • Budget and spend data
  • Conversion tracking configuration
  • Account structure and settings
  • Change history

How we use your data

We use your advertising data solely to provide the CampaignOwl service — specifically to audit your accounts, identify issues and opportunities, generate health scores and recommendations, and apply optimisations that you approve.

We do not sell, rent, or share your advertising data with any third parties. Your data is used exclusively to provide the service to you.

Google Ads API — Limited Use Disclosure

CampaignOwl's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically, CampaignOwl:

  • Only uses Google Ads data to provide and improve the CampaignOwl service for you
  • Does not transfer Google Ads data to third parties unless necessary to provide the service, required by law, or with your explicit consent
  • Does not use Google Ads data for serving advertisements
  • Does not allow humans to read your Google Ads data unless you provide affirmative consent, it is necessary for security purposes, or it is required by law

Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use secure cloud infrastructure with access controls, encryption at rest and in transit, and regular security reviews.

We retain your advertising data only for as long as your account is active. When you disconnect your advertising accounts or delete your CampaignOwl account, we delete your stored advertising data within 30 days.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law (UK GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Disconnect your advertising accounts at any time

Cookies

Our website currently does not use cookies or tracking technologies. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at hello@campaignowl.com.
